## ğŸ¯ Best Practices

1. **Always use customer's name** - personalizes the interaction
2. **Reference lead_id** - helps tracking and professionalism
3. **Offer 2-3 time slots** - increases booking rate
4. **Confirm address explicitly** - avoids wasted trips
5. **Set clear expectations** - pricing, timing, guarantees
6. **Update status immediately** - keeps queue clean
7. **Use emojis sparingly** - professional but friendly
8. **Respond within SLA** - maintains priority scoring
